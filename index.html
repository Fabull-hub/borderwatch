ABOUT BORDERTREND

Global Smuggling & Border Security Intelligence

BorderTrend is a real-time intelligence platform aggregating open-source reporting on smuggling, trafficking, border security, and related enforcement activity from over 70 official, investigative, and media sources worldwide.

Our mission is to make border security intelligence accessible to researchers, journalists, law enforcement professionals, policy makers, and trade compliance practitioners.

All content is sourced from publicly available RSS feeds and news APIs. BorderTrend does not produce original reporting and links directly to original sources.
